This dataset contains historical and forecasted price data for major precious metals, with a primary focus on silver and comparative coverage of gold and platinum. 
The data is designed to support time-series analysis, economic research, and machine learning–based forecasting of commodity prices under changing market conditions.

The historical component spans the last ten years of daily price observations, capturing long-term trends, volatility regimes, and cyclical movements across metals. 
To support medium-term decision making and research use cases, the dataset also includes weekly aggregated series and forward-looking projections extending to March 2026.

The dataset reflects periods of rapid price appreciation, market corrections, and structural shifts influenced by macroeconomic conditions, monetary policy, industrial demand, and investor behavior. 
Special attention is given to recent price surges and subsequent pullbacks, allowing users to examine momentum dynamics and post-rally adjustments.
