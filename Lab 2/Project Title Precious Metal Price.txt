Project Title: Precious Metal Price Prediction

Problem Statement: This project focuses on a Regression problem. The dataset contains historical financial data for precious metals including Gold, Silver, and Platinum.

Target Variable: Gold_Price (The value we want to predict).

Input Features: Silver_Price, Platinum_Price, Gold_Volatility_30d, and Moving Averages.

Goal: The model will learn the relationship between different precious metal markets to predict the price of Gold based on other market indicators and historical trends.
